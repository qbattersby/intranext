An innovative way to protect customers’ personally identifiable
information (PII) during calls with your contact center agents.

When customers input or speak sensitive information on a call, iGuard prevents the customer service representative (CSR) from seeing or hearing the data. Your customers AND your agents are protected.

Process customers more efficiently with reduced transaction times.

Customers expect their sensitive information to be protected and processed as quickly as possible. The iGuard architecture leverages existing infrastructure and allows your customers to enter sensitive account information during a call—with the CSR still on the line.

The CSR can then quickly verify and submit the information to your back-end systems without ever being exposed to your customer’s sensitive information. iGuard reduces average transaction times between 7 to 15 seconds per call.

We take security so seriously that we exceed pci standards.

As a PA-DSS Validated application, iGuard prevents sensitive data from traversing across applications, the network and throughout your telephony environment.

iGuard exceeds contact center PCI protection standards by eliminating Personal Identifiable Information (PII) exposure to the ACD, call recording system, agent, agent desktop, desktop application and servers residing on the network. iGuard provides compliance and audit protection.

iGuard reduces your costs in multiple areas.

iGuard is easy to implement, reducing operational costs achieving compliance in weeks and ROI within months. iGuard’s software only solution is simple to scale leveraging the existing infrastructure.

With iGuard’s agile pay-as-you-go model you can replace upfront capital expense with low variable cost and pay only for what you use. There is no need to pay upfront for excess capacity or get penalized for underplanning.

Guard your brand by guarding your customer's sensitive information.

iGuard protects your customer, saves time and drives customer satisfaction. iGuard eliminates the need to transfer the customer to an automated system or route to another organization to manage sensitive information. iGuard preserves first call resolution and improves customer satisfaction.

With iGuard customers will never have to share sensitive information aloud, they will use their keypad to securely enter the information. Customers embrace this safe and efficient approach to managing their private information.

Improve customer satisfaction rates.

Happy customers are ones that spend less time waiting for help, have their transactions handled quickly and efficiently, and feel that their information is being protected. iGuard helps make all of this possible.

Industry Leading Compliance

Your customers' trust is valuable, so we ensure that we meet and exceed industry compliance.

How iGuard Works

iGuard is as an extension of IntraNext’s proven computer telephony integration (CTI) software. It runs on customer-provided hardware within the call center’s network and telephony infrastructure. No sensitive information is stored within the iGuard application.

iGuard works with many call center systems, including:

  • Avaya and Cisco ACD phone systems
  • Most quality management applications, including those offered by Calabrio, NICE, and Verint
  • Leading payment gateways
  • Citrix and Microsoft Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) environments
