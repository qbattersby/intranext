Our Story

The original founders, all of whom were spending our days building, integrating and managing large contact centers, thought that there should be a better way to do things. The ideas that we sketched out back in 1996 remain at the heart of what IntraNext Systems does today: providing best-of-breed computer telephony integration solutions for medium and large contact centers.

When we started out, we knew there were other CTI application providers. But the thought that sustained us as we moved from idea to development to solution was simple: We can do it better. We believe we have, and we certainly have the presence that helps support this belief. Today, you can find our software on over 35,000 desktops in some of the largest contact centers in the telecommunications industry.

Along the way, we’ve made customer service a priority, since we believe in the idea that you should treat others as you wish to be treated. We’ve also focused on building a company that believes in giving back. To that end, we have hosted numerous charity golf tournaments benefiting Children’s Hospital as well as contributing too many other philanthropic causes. There’s no doubt that we play hard. But we work even harder, and have attracted talent to our organization who make improving contact center efficiencies a priority. Today, we have company stakeholders with extensive experience in call centers and the telecommunications industry
